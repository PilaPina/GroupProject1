ABOUT US

Five years ago, a group of passionate individuals who shared a deep love for the untamed beauty of Iceland came together and created an incredible tour guide company. Their adventure-seeking spirits led them to explore rugged mountains, scenic trails, and majestic glaciers. As they ventured into the world of ice caves and glaciers, they were captivated and knew they had found their true calling. Their mission expanded beyond just providing experiences; they wanted to educate, inspire, and protect the delicate beauty of nature. In addition to offering thrilling adventures, they also focus on conservation, teaching about safety and the impact of climate change. This team of guides strives to create a lasting bond between people and Iceland's glaciers, fostering a sense of wonder and love for the natural world. Get ready to immerse yourself in its icy wonders and develop a deep connection that will stay with you forever.
